## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the app's Settings screen. Navigate to **Settings → Delete Account** and confirm. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data.

### How do I reset my password?

SliceFocus uses Sign in with Apple and Google Sign-In — there is no separate password to reset. Manage your credentials through your Apple ID or Google account settings.

### My data isn't syncing across devices

Ensure you are signed in with the same account on all devices and that you have an active internet connection. If the issue persists, try signing out and back in.
